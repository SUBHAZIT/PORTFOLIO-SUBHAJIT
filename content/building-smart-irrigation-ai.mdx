# BUILDING A SMART IRRIGATION SYSTEM WITH DRONES & AI

DURING THE **TEXIBITION 2K24 BRAINWARE UNIVERSITY TECHFEST**, MY TEAM AND I EMBARKED ON A MISSION TO SOLVE A REAL-WORLD AGRICULTURAL PROBLEM: INEFFICIENT WATER USAGE IN FARMING. WE BUILT A "SMART IRRIGATION SYSTEM" POWERED BY DRONES AND ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE.

## THE CONCEPT

TRADITIONAL IRRIGATION SYSTEMS OFTEN WASTE WATER BY BLINDLY SPRAYING ENTIRE FIELDS REGARDLESS OF THE ACTUAL SOIL MOISTURE. OUR SOLUTION INVOLVED DEPLOYING A CUSTOM-BUILT DRONE EQUIPPED WITH COMPUTER VISION AND SENSORS TO FLY OVER FIELDS, ANALYZE THE TERRAIN, AND IDENTIFY EXACTLY WHICH SECTIONS NEEDED HYDRATION.

## THE TECHNOLOGY STACK

TO MAKE THIS A REALITY WITHIN A SHORT TIMEFRAME, WE USED:
- **PYTHON & OPENCV**: FOR REAL-TIME IMAGE PROCESSING FROM THE DRONE CAMERA.
- **TENSORFLOW**: TO DEPLOY A LIGHTWEIGHT MACHINE LEARNING MODEL THAT CLASSIFIES SOIL DRYNESS BASED ON VISUAL DATA.
- **SOCKET.IO & REACT**: FOR THE GROUND CONTROL DASHBOARD, ALLOWING US TO MONITOR THE DRONE'S LIVE FEED AND MANUALLY OVERRIDE WATER PUMPS IF NECESSARY.

## OVERCOMING CHALLENGES

THE HARDEST PART WAS ESTABLISHING A RELIABLE REAL-TIME DATA TRANSMISSION LINK BETWEEN THE DRONE AND OUR GROUND SERVER WITHOUT SIGNIFICANT LATENCY. BY OPTIMIZING OUR SOCKET CONNECTIONS AND COMPRESSING THE VIDEO FEED, WE MANAGED TO ACHIEVE LESS THAN 200MS DELAY.

## THE RESULT

IT WAS AN EYE-OPENING EXPERIENCE TO WITNESS HOW IDEAS TURN INTO REAL PRODUCTS THROUGH TEAMWORK, CREATIVITY, AND DEDICATION. SEEING THE DRONE SUCCESSFULLY IDENTIFY A DRY PATCH AND TRIGGER THE IRRIGATION VALVES AUTOMATICALLY FURTHER STRENGTHENED MY PASSION FOR BUILDING IMPACTFUL TECHNOLOGY.
